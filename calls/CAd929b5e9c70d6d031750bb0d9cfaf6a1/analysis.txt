SCENARIO PROMPT
================
Current scenario: office hours question. You are calling to ask if the office is open after 5 PM and whether they offer evening appointments; if not, ask for the next available morning slot.

================

The agent performed identity verification (asked DOB), noted a mismatch but proceeded “for demo purposes.” After repeated prompting, the agent answered that evening appointments are available on Wednesdays, with hours from 12 PM to 7 PM. The subsequent attempt to schedule was not completed due to a stated system issue, but scheduling was not required to fulfill the scenario’s goal.
