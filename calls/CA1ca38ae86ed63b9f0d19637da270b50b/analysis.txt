SCENARIO PROMPT
================
Current scenario: medication refill. You are calling to refill your asthma inhaler and ask whether the doctor needs to approve it first because the pharmacy says you are out.


ANALYSIS
================
Call Summary:
The patient (Alex) called to request a refill of their albuterol (albuterol sulfate) asthma inhaler and asked whether a doctor needs to approve the refill since they are out. The agent verified DOB and callback number, gathered pharmacy information with back-and-forth clarification, and identified the Walgreens at 2945 S 6th St, Springfield, IL 62703 as the chosen pharmacy. The agent documented the refill request and stated it was sent to the clinic support team, indicating the staff will follow up.

Scenario Outcome:
Goal completed: Partially
The agent captured the refill request and pharmacy details and said the request was sent to clinic staff. However, the agent did not directly answer whether a doctor must approve the refill when the patient asked, and did not provide a clear timeframe for when the refill would be processed or available. The agent also did not explicitly confirm “Yes, we will send it to that Walgreens” after the patient asked for that confirmation.

Source: Agent

Expected Behavior: Clearly explain the approval process (e.g., if no refills remain, the provider must approve) and what that means for timing/next steps.

Severity: Medium
Source: Agent
Where: 16:22:59, 16:24:39–16:24:52
Details: When the patient asked when to expect the refill at the pharmacy, the agent provided only vague statements (“as soon as they can”) and a process note, not an estimated turnaround (e.g., same day vs. 1–2 business days).
Expected Behavior: Provide a typical processing window and clarify that the patient will be notified if delays occur.

Source: Agent

Details: The agent collected the medication name but did not confirm strength/form (e.g., albuterol HFA 90 mcg) or quantity, which many clinics verify to prevent processing delays.

Polite and cooperative on both sides, though the agent’s responses were somewhat indirect and repetitive.

Final Recommendation:
Partially successful. The request was captured and routed with a pharmacy identified, but clearer answers on approval requirements, an expected timeframe, and explicit final confirmation to the named pharmacy are needed for a fully successful refill call.
